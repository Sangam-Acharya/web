Planets Remedies (ग्रह शान्ति उपायहरू)

Sun (Surya) (सूर्य)

To strengthen the Sun, offer water (Arghya) to the rising Sun every morning while chanting its mantra. Donate red cloth, wheat, jaggery, or copper on Sundays. Respect and serve your father and government officials. Visit Surya temples regularly.
Mantra:
ॐ घृणिः सूर्याय नमः
Om Ghrinih Suryaya Namah

Moon (Chandra) (चन्द्र)

To pacify the Moon, worship Lord Shiva and offer water or milk to the Shiva Linga on Mondays. Donate white clothes, rice, and milk. Spend time with your mother and focus on emotional well-being.
Mantra:
ॐ चन्द्राय नमः
Om Chandraaya Namah

Mars (Mangal) (मंगल)

Worship Lord Hanuman or Kartikeya on Tuesdays to balance Mars energy. Recite Hanuman Chalisa or offer red flowers and sweets. Donate red lentils, coral, or copper items. Practicing patience is key to channeling Mars positively.
Mantra:
ॐ अङ्गारकाय नमः
Om Angarakaya Namah

Mercury (Budh) (बुध)

To strengthen Mercury, worship Lord Vishnu or Ganesha on Wednesdays. Donate green vegetables, green clothes, or moong dal. Improve communication and avoid lying or gossiping.
Mantra:
ॐ बुं बुधाय नमः
Om Bum Budhaya Namah

Jupiter (Guru) (गुरु)

To please Jupiter, worship Brihaspati or Lord Vishnu on Thursdays. Donate turmeric, yellow cloth, and books. Respect your teachers and elders. Engage in spiritual learning and avoid arrogance.
Mantra:
ॐ बृं बृहस्पतये नमः
Om Brim Brihaspataye Namah

Venus (Shukra) (शुक्र)

To enhance Venus, worship Goddess Lakshmi on Fridays. Keep surroundings clean and donate fragrant items, ghee, white clothes, or sweets. Value love, beauty, and art without becoming indulgent.
Mantra:
ॐ शुक्राय नमः
Om Shukraaya Namah

Saturn (Shani) (शनि)

To reduce Saturn’s harsh effects, worship Lord Shani or Hanuman on Saturdays. Offer mustard oil, black sesame, or iron items. Serve the poor and elderly. Practice discipline, humility, and simplicity.
Mantra:
ॐ शं शनैश्चराय नमः
Om Sham Shanaischaraya Namah

Rahu (North Node) (राहु)

To balance Rahu, worship Goddess Durga or Bhairava. Chant Durga Kavach or Rahu Beej mantra. Donate black blankets, mustard oil, or sesame seeds. Avoid deceit and cultivate mindfulness.
Mantra:
ॐ रां राहवे नमः
Om Raam Rahave Namah

Ketu (South Node) (केतु)

To calm Ketu, worship Lord Ganesha. Chant Ganesha Atharvashirsha or Ketu mantra. Donate multi-colored cloth, dogs, or blankets. Pursue meditation and spiritual growth over material attachments.
Mantra:
ॐ कें केतवे नमः
Om Kem Ketave Namah
